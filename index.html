TimeTravel: A multimodal prehistoric and historic benchmark for LMMs

A visual overview of TimeTravel, evaluating LMMs across 10 historical regions and 266 cultures. On the right, sample artifacts highlight the dataset's diversity.

Abstract

Understanding historical and cultural artifacts demands human expertise and computational techniques, yet the process remains complex. TimeTravel is a benchmark of 10,250 expert-verified samples spanning 266 cultures across 10 regions. Designed for AI-driven analysis of manuscripts, artworks, inscriptions, and archaeological discoveries, it provides a structured dataset and evaluation framework.
